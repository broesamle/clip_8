2.4  Move to location (absolute)

Move to an absolute location. (1) An arrow-shaped polyline has its head at `p0`. (2) Its shape and orientation indicates, which part of the object (centre, edge, etc.) to move to the destination. (3) The destination is at `p0`.

Move a set of objects with the lower left edge to the absolute location, `p0`. [normal_execution 2]
