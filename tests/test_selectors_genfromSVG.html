1.2  Selectors

Select sets of objects.

Selectors are attached to instructions in in order to define a selection (set) of object(s).

How this set is used depends on the instruction. Typically, the selection defines those objects that will be affected by the instruction.

For this section, selectors are presented in isolation, without any instruction. The point of attachment, otherwise appearent by the instruction, will be indicated by a small arrow.

Similarly, the selection is indicated by colour for presentation purposes.
